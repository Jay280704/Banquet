Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries regarding bookings, catering structures, rules, and services.

What is the total capacity of Masala Mantra Kamothe Banquet Hall?

Masala Mantra accommodates events of various scales. The Imperial Ballroom hosts up to 1,200 guests, while the Royal Meadows Lawns can host up to 1,500 guests. For boutique gatherings, the Zenith Lounge is perfect for 80 to 200 guests.

Do you allow outside catering and decorator agencies?

We boast an award-winning in-house team of culinary experts and visual designers. However, to accommodate specific religious requirements or pre-contracted designers, we do allow outside caterers and decorators upon payment of a minor royalty surcharge.

What is the advance booking and cancellation policy?

We require a 25% non-refundable advance deposit to confirm and block your date. The remaining 50% is due 3 months prior to the event, and the final 25% balance must be settled 10 days before the celebration date.

Is there an electricity backup facility in case of outages?

Yes, we have 100% silent DG power generator backups with automatic transfer switches that trigger within 3 seconds, ensuring your audio-visual, lighting, and air-conditioning systems operate uninterrupted.

Can we book rooms for out-of-town guests?

Yes! Masala Mantra features 12 luxurious executive guest suites, including a complimentary grand bridal dressing suite equipped with professional studio lighting, lounge sofas, and dedicated security lockers.
